---

When you open a run—usually by clicking a row on **Sessions** or after **Test run**—you land on the **Run inspector**. Layout varies by release, but you typically get:

- **Header** area with **status**, **timing**, **tokens**, and which **agent version** ran.
- **Trace** or **timeline** of steps (model, tools, sub-agent calls). The density and layout follow your **[Settings → General → Session trace layout](/product/dashboard/settings#general)** preference (**Basic mode** vs **Advanced mode**).
- **Conversation** or structured **input/output** views.
- **Sub-agent** sections when child agents participated.
- **Pause** or **review** panels when HITL or Guard stopped the run for a person.

## Triggered by

The screen usually states **how** the run started: **Test run**, a **named trigger**, a **schedule**, an **event**, or similar. That matches the **Triggered by** column on **Sessions**.

## Status badges

Use the **status** badge to see whether the run **succeeded**, is still **running**, **failed**, or is **waiting** on a human. **Paused** states tie to **HITL** or **Guard** review.

## Plan / overage hints

If your organization uses **session overage** or special billing labels, extra text or badges may appear on the run or session—wording depends on your Phrony version and plan.
