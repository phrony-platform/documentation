---
title: "Dashboard home"
description: "Short description of the analytics home page."
---

**Dashboard** in the sidebar opens **home**: period **metrics** (agents, sessions, failures, tokens, live counts), a **token usage** chart by model, and **Recent sessions** with filters and optional **Mark complete** / **Terminate session**. Shown for **Owner**, **Admin**, and **Developer** only.

If the workspace exceeds plan limits after a plan change, a **plan compliance** banner may block new runs until usage is fixed—see **[Limits & plans](/product/reference/platform-rules)**.
