**Guard** (sidebar, sometimes **Beta**) opens **Anomaly control**: **Rule proposals** (accept/reject suggested rules) and **Incidents**. Accepting a proposal creates a **Testing** **[agent version](/product/concepts/agent-version)**. **L1** rules are edited per version under **Limits & Safety**. See **[Agent version](/product/concepts/agent-version)** and **[Limits & plans](/product/reference/platform-rules)**.
