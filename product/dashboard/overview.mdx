title: "Dashboard overview"
description: "What the Phrony dashboard is and how navigation is organized."
---

The **Phrony dashboard** is the web app where you manage **[agents](/product/concepts/agent)**, **[integrations](/product/concepts/integration)**, **[secrets](/product/concepts/secret)**, **[LLM providers](/product/concepts/llm-provider)**, **[triggers](/product/concepts/trigger)**, and review **[sessions](/product/concepts/session)** and **[runs](/product/concepts/run)**. Most tasks are **not** available to the **Operator** role, which instead uses **[Approvals](/product/dashboard/approvals)**.

**Sidebar (typical builder):** **Dashboard** (home analytics), **Secret Manager**, **LLM Providers**, **Agents**, **Integrations**, **Guard** (when enabled), **Review → Approvals**, **Settings**, **Get Help**. **Quick Create** (**+**) jumps to new secret, provider, agent, or integration.

Details: **[Limits & plans](/product/reference/platform-rules)** (roles and caps). Screen-specific blurbs: **[Dashboard home](/product/dashboard/home)**, **[Settings](/product/dashboard/settings)**, **[Approvals](/product/dashboard/approvals)**, **[Guard](/product/dashboard/guard-anomaly)**.
