After you sign in, the **sidebar** is your main map. What appears there depends on your **workspace role** (see **[Limits, permissions, and plans](/product/reference/platform-rules#workspace-roles-in-the-dashboard)**).

**Operators** see a reduced sidebar: **Review → Approvals** and the footer links (**Settings**, **Get Help**). The main nav hides **Dashboard** (analytics home), **Secret Manager**, **LLM Providers**, **Agents**, **Integrations**, and **Guard**—those are for **Developer** and above.

## Quick Create

At the top of the sidebar, the **+** (**Quick Create**) menu jumps straight to common tasks:

- **New Secret** — opens **Secret Manager** with the add dialog ready.
- **New LLM Provider** — opens **LLM Providers** with the create flow.
- **New Agent** — opens **Agents** with **New Agent** ready.
- **New Integration** — opens **Integrations** with the create flow.

If your role cannot create an item, you may not see the entry or the dialog will explain that the action is not allowed.

## Main sections (typical builder roles)

- **Dashboard** — **[Dashboard home](/product/dashboard/home)** metrics, token chart, and **Recent sessions** (not shown to **Operator**).
- **Secret Manager** — list, add, and remove secrets (add/remove for **Owner** / **Admin** only).
- **LLM Providers** — list and inspect for all builders; **edit** for **Owner** / **Admin**.
- **Agents** — list, open an agent, create, edit, delete (delete rules depend on live versions).
- **Integrations** — HTTP and built-in services your agent versions can call.
- **Guard** — when your workspace has it, **[Guard & anomaly control](/product/dashboard/guard-anomaly)** (may show a **Beta** label).

## Review

Under the **Review** group:

- **Approvals** — pending sign-offs, **AITL escalations**, and **Anomaly** tasks; see **[Approvals](/product/dashboard/approvals)**. The nav item can show a pending count.

## Plan compliance

When the workspace exceeds plan limits after a plan change, a top-of-page alert can block new runs until usage is fixed. Details: **[Dashboard home](/product/dashboard/home#plan-compliance-banner)** and **[Limits, permissions, and plans](/product/reference/platform-rules)**.

## Footer of the sidebar

- **Settings** — **[Settings](/product/dashboard/settings)** tabs: **General** (appearance, session trace layout), **Workspace**, **Plan & billing** (billing roles), **API keys**.
- **Get Help** — opens **Get help**: community (**Join Discord**) and **Email** (`support@phrony.com` in the product UI) for account, billing, or technical issues.

## Integrations and API keys

**Integrations** lists every **service** and its **operations** you can attach on an agent’s **Operations** tab. Creating connectors may be limited by your **plan** (maximum custom connectors, etc.).

Under **Settings → API keys** (wording may vary), you create keys for **automation outside the browser**. Trigger history may show that a run started via an **API** key in plain language (for example “API” or the key’s label).
