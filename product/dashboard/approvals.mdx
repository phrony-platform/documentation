**Review → Approvals** lists tasks that need sign-off (**Pending** / **Completed**), filterable by kind (**Approvals**, **AITL escalations**, **Anomaly**). Rows link into **[Run](/product/concepts/run)** detail when available. Full product copy lives in the UI.
