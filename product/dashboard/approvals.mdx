---
title: "Approvals"
description: "Review queue under Review → Approvals."
---

**Review → Approvals** lists tasks that need sign-off (**Pending** / **Completed**), filterable by kind (**Approvals**, **AITL escalations**, **Anomaly**). Rows link into **[Run](/product/concepts/run)** detail when available. Full product copy lives in the UI. **AITL escalations** are part of **[Agent in the loop](/product/agent-in-the-loop/overview)** when a **HITL** parent escalates a Sub-agent question to a human.
