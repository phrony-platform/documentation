## Run status

While a **child** waits on the **parent** (or human), runs may show states such as **Waiting for parent** / **Awaiting reply** (labels vary by release). The **parent** **root** **[run](/product/concepts/run)** trace is the usual place to see **AITL** steps and controls to **respond** or **escalate**.

**[Session](/product/concepts/session)** rows may stay **Running** or move to **Awaiting**-style statuses until all pending coordination finishes.

## Approvals

**[Review → Approvals](/product/dashboard/approvals)** can include tasks of kind **AITL escalations** when a **HITL** **parent** escalates a child’s question to a **human**. Operators resolve these like other review tasks; rows may deep-link into **Run** detail. See **[Human escalation](/product/agent-in-the-loop/human-escalation)** for the conceptual flow.

**HITL** **integration** approvals (operation gates) are a separate kind from **AITL** escalations, though both can show in the same queue filters.

## Inspectors and permissions

Who may **respond** on the parent run vs who may **approve** an escalation follows workspace **[roles](/product/reference/platform-rules)**. **Operators** often act on **Approvals**; builders typically configure agents and versions.
