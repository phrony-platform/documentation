## Description

A **workspace** is the boundary for your team’s data and settings in Phrony. [Agents](/concepts/agent), [secrets](/concepts/secret), [LLM providers](/concepts/llm-provider), [sessions](/concepts/session), and [integrations](/concepts/integration) each belong to **one** workspace so your work stays isolated from other customers.

| Role of a workspace    | Details                                                         |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **What you switch**    | The workspace switcher picks which team environment you are in. |
| **Membership**         | Where you **invite** people and assign **roles**.               |
| **Billing and limits** | **Subscription**, usage, and caps apply **per workspace**.      |

## Properties

What defines a workspace in the product:

| What you see       | Description                                                                                               |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Name**           | The label for your team or environment (shown in the switcher and settings).                              |
| **Status**         | Whether the workspace is active and available, or limited (for example, suspended) based on your account. |
| **Members**        | People who can sign in, each with a **role** that controls what they can do.                              |
| **Plan and usage** | Your current subscription tier and how much of the included capacity you are using (including **file storage** for the [file library](/concepts/file-library) when your plan includes it). |

#### Roles

| Role                 | Usually can                                                                                           |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Owner**, **Admin** | Billing, members, sensitive configuration.                                                            |
| **Developer**        | Design [agents](/concepts/agent), [integrations](/concepts/integration), [secrets](/concepts/secret). |
| **Operator**         | Run and review work without full design access.                                                       |

Exact names and permissions follow what your Phrony dashboard shows for your org.

## Notes

- While you are signed in, the Phrony dashboard is scoped to **one** active workspace at a time; use the workspace switcher to change it.
- Usage and limits ([agents](/concepts/agent), [LLM providers](/concepts/llm-provider), [session](/concepts/session) volume) are **per workspace**, based on your plan and add-ons.
- One person can be a member of **multiple** workspaces; switch between them from the account menu.
- **File** attachments for agent runs are stored in the [file library](/concepts/file-library) for that workspace (quotas apply per plan).
